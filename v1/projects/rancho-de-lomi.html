Rancho de Lomi

2012 / Client / Poster / Illustration

The Wedding

I was asked to design a poster for Yvette & Mathew's wedding invite. We then followed up with several designs for wedding materials to compliment the invitation.

Yvette is an incredibly passionate horse veterinarian. For the table cards, she wished to assign each group a horse, raher than a number. The idea being that in looking for your table, you might also learn a little about your horse.

The Crest

Yvette & Mathew (who may or may not be my stepmother and father, respectively) asked me to design a crest that reflected their combined heritages - a bed of tulips representing Yvette's Dutch origins, and a Mayan calendar reflecting Mathew's origins in Central America. The crest has become something of a branding element in the Lomas-Nout clan, with the icon appearing on beer & wine glasses, running jerseys, and a tile on the entrance to their home.

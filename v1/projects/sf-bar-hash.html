San Francisco Hash & BAR Hash

2011 / Client / Illustration / Apparel

The San Francisco Crest

I was initially contacted by the San Francisco Hash House Harriers to redesign their previous crest. The results of that were decidedly mixed, so I created an alternative to present them. They loved it.

The BAR Anniversy Shirt

Through the people I met at SFH3 (my father), I was contacted to create a shirt for another hash. This one was to celebrate their 50th run. BAR Hash uses the BART system in San Francisco to get to the runs, so I incorporated the BART map into an infographic-style design representing the amount of times they ran from each stop.

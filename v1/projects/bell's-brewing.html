Bell’s Brewing

2013 / Student / Branding / Advertising / Web

The Logo

Bell’s Beer is a craft brewery from Kalamazoo, Michigan (loacated in nearby Comstock now). However, their current logo looks, to me, oddly Spanish. When I was tasked with redesigning the company’s identity, the first thing I decided was to make use of the pride they take in being a Michigan‐based company. So I created the mitten-bell.

The Brands

Bell’s treats each brew as it’s own brand. I thought that was a clever solution to the problem many breweries face when they brew as diverse a range of beers as Bell’s. However, I decided to try and make these different brands feel part of a cohesive whole through line-thickness and the use of the mitten-bell as a design element. Each brand receives it’s own spot color, and each label is a screenprinted version of the brand crest.

The Collateral

In addition to the brands, I created an advertisement and a wesite mockup for this reimagined Bell’s. The Bell’s website is constantly updated, and places a large emphasis on events taking place at both Bell’s own Eccentric Café and others in the region related to the brewery. I took these consideratons in mind and created a light, readable website that is easily updated, either manually or with a CMS.

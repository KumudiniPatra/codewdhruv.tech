2010 - 2011 / Personal / Illustration

The Collages

One day, I came upon a vendor at my local antique mall selling old magazines. I immediately picked up around five issues of LIFE from the 1960’s and scanned them at 600dpi. Eventually I started playing about with them, using them mostly as textures at first, and then as the focus later. This is the result of the latter round of experiments.

Lenny’s Burger

2013 / Student / Branding / Advertising / Web

The Logo

Lenny’s Burger is an amazing burger shop located in my home of Phoenix, Arizona. Unfortunately, their image in recent years has become this creepy man‐baby with odd type that wouldn’t be out of place on a bowling shirt (not that there's anything inherently wrong with that). When tasked to redesign the brand, my first move was to bring the logo back to the feeling of the original script logo.

The Identity

My next step was to find a voice for the brand. The script was friendly, but if I took it the wrong direction, I feared it could come off as pretentious. To undercut that, I imbued the website and advertising with a tongue‐in‐cheek sense of humor. I also tried to make the paper menu feel like the kind you’d see at any great local restaurant.

The Website

I had the good fortune of having both a web design and JavaScript choice project due at the same time as the identity. I took the oppurtunity to flex my development skills and create the website from the mockup I created for my graphic design class. You can view the website here.
